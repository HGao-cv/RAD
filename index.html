Framework

RAD takes a three-stage training paradigm. In the perception pre-training, ground-truths of map and agent are used to guide instance-level tokens to encode corresponding information. In the planning pre-training stage, large-scale driving demonstrations are used to initialize the action distribution. In the planning post-training stage, RL and IL synergistically finetune the AD policy.
